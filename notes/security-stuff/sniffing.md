- Protocols for easy sniffing: 
  - TELNET
  - HTTP
  - SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol)
  - NNTP (Network News Transfer Protocol)
  - POP (Post Office Protocol)
  - FTP 
  - IMAP (Internet Message Access Protocol)

- Tools:
  - Wireshark (tshark cli)
  - tcpdump
  - windump
  - dsniff
  - ettercap
